Pasta al Limone e Basilico

Gennaro Contaldo's famous lemon pasta.

Ingredients


  • 500g dried linguine
  • 3 Sicilian lemons
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 75g Parmesan cheese
  • 75g Pecorino cheese
  • 1 bunch of fresh basil
  • 1 handful of rocket

Method


  1. Cook the linguine in a pan of boiling salted water according to the packet intructions, then drain and return to the pan.
  2. Meanwhile, finely grate the zest from 1 lemon into a bowl, then squeeze in the juice from all three.
  3. Drizzle in 6 tablespoons of oil and finely grate in the Parmesan and Pecorino. Beat together - it'll go thick and creamy.
  4. Season to taste with sea salt and black pepper, add extra lemon juice if needed. Pick and finely chop the basil leaves.
  5. Add the lemony sauce to the linguine and shake the pan to coat each strand of pasta.
  6. Finish by stirring through the chopped basil and rocket.
